Gourmet Burgers

Indulge in our Gourmet Burgers, carefully crafted using the finest cuts of premium meats, perfectly seasoned and flame-grilled to perfection. Every burger is topped with fresh, locally sourced produce, artisanal cheeses, and our signature house-made sauces that bring layers of flavor. Pair it with a side of crispy golden fries and elevate your meal to a moment of pure indulgence.

Classic Fish & Chips

Relish the timeless delight of our Classic Fish & Chips. Prepared with hand-cut, flaky white fish, coated in our secret-recipe batter, and fried to golden perfection. The result? A crisp, flavorful crust that gives way to tender, melt-in-your-mouth fish.

Served alongside thick-cut, golden fries seasoned just right, this dish strikes the perfect balance of tradition, quality, and flavor. Complete your meal with our house-made tartar sauce or a splash of fresh lemon juice for a truly authentic experience.

  • Freshly Sourced, Sustainable Fish
  • Signature Crispy Batter
  • Perfectly Cooked Golden Fries
